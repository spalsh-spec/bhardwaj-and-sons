## 8. The single hardest discipline of social-brand-building

**Don't post every day.** Don't engagement-bait. Don't use trending audio. Don't do "founder day in the life" content. Don't fight other founders in comments.

**Hermès posts on Instagram about twice a week. Cartier posts about three times a week. Both have ~12M and ~8M followers respectively.** They earn it by saying nothing unnecessary.

The whole strategy: publish *only* when you have something worth saying, in a format that matches the brand voice, and let the spaces between posts do the heavy work of signalling that you're not desperate for attention.

This is the same discipline as the methodology itself: ship the work, let it speak, don't talk over it.
